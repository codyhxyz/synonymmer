### Added
- Initial release.
- `synonymmer` skill — given a seed word/phrase/concept, outputs 100–200 related words organized into 12–18 vibe-labeled clusters (archetypes, single-syllable snares, verbs-as-names, Latin/Greek pulls, wild cards, and more).
